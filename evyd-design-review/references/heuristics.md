# EVYD UX & Visual Design Heuristic Rating Sheet

来源：EVYD 内部 UX 评估框架（Confluence）

评审时逐条对照，为每一条标记状态：
- **✅ OK** — 这个维度没有明显问题
- **⚠️ 待改进** — 用户反馈中有对应困惑或障碍
- **➖ 未涉及** — 当前设计/截图中不涉及该维度

---

## 10 条核心 Heuristics

**1. System Feedback（系统反馈）**
The app should give clear messages or notifications after users complete an action — like booking, logging a routine, or submitting information — so they know it worked.
用户完成操作后，系统有没有给出明确的反馈？用户能知道操作成功了吗？

**2. Clarity & Language（清晰度与语言）**
The app's words, icons, and visuals should make sense and feel relatable to users. The meaning should be clear without needing extra explanation.
页面上的文字、图标和视觉元素，目标用户能看懂吗？不需要额外解释吗？

**3. User Interaction & CTA（用户交互与行动按钮）**
Users should easily know what they can do — like tapping, selecting, or confirming. Buttons and actions should be clear, visible, and respond properly when used.
用户能清楚地知道"我可以在这里做什么"吗？按钮和可交互区域是否足够明显？

**4. Consistency（一致性）**
The design style, layout, and wording should be uniform across the app — whether it's buttons, banners, or pages — so everything feels like part of the same system.
不同页面/组件的风格、布局和措辞是否一致？用户有没有感到"怎么突然变了"？

**5. Error Handling & Prevention（错误处理与预防）**
If users make a mistake, they should be able to fix or undo it easily. The app should also prevent errors using confirmations, disabled buttons, or helpful messages.
用户出错时能容易地纠正吗？系统有没有机制帮助用户避免或恢复错误？

**6. Efficiency（效率）**
Common actions like booking, logging data, or submitting forms should be quick and easy with minimal steps. The process should feel smooth and straightforward.
完成核心任务需要几步？流程是否顺畅？有没有不必要的步骤让用户觉得繁琐？

**7. Guidance & Support（引导与支持）**
Provide short hints, tooltips, or brief instructions when users might need help or are unsure what to do next.
在用户可能卡住的地方，有没有提示、说明或引导帮助他们继续？

**8. Visual Focus（视觉焦点）**
The most important information or actions should stand out immediately when users open a screen.
用户打开页面时，最重要的信息或操作是否第一眼就能看到？

**9. Spacing & Layout（间距与布局）**
Content should have enough space to breathe and look organized — not too cramped or messy.
页面内容是否有足够空间，看起来整洁有序，不拥挤？

**10. Hierarchy（层级）**
Text size, color, and placement should clearly guide users on what to read or do first.
文字大小、颜色和位置是否清晰地引导用户"先看什么、再做什么"？

---

## 11. Other Suggestions（其他建议）

This will be used when all 10 heuristics don't apply to the problem. Such as products, modules or requirements that need to be adjusted or changed.
当以上 10 条都不适用时，在这里记录其他结构性或产品层面的建议。

---

## 评分说明

- 每条只需标注状态 + 一行观察，不需要展开分析
- 观察内容应来自用户反馈部分已发现的问题，不要另外创造新发现
- 如果用户反馈中没有覆盖某条 heuristic，标 **➖ 未涉及**，不要强行打分
